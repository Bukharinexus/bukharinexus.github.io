Our Business

Al Makkah General Store, a subsidiary of Bukhari NEXUS Pvt Ltd, provides essential household and commercial grocery supplies. We serve families, restaurants, and small retailers with a focus on quality, affordability, and consistent availability.

Product Categories

Grocery: Rice, Flour, Sugar, Spices, Oil, Lentils.
Beverages: Soft drinks, Juices, Bottled water, Tea.
Household: Cleaning supplies, Laundry, Kitchenware.
Personal Care: Skincare, Soap, Shampoo, Hygiene products.

Strategic Future Growth

We are actively working toward transforming our operations into a regional powerhouse:

  • Developing a robust regional distribution network.
  • Expanding into FMCG supply and dealership programs.
  • Investing in warehouse infrastructure for bulk storage.
  • Implementing digital ordering systems for our B2B partners.
