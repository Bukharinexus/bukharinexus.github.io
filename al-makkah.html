Operations Overview

Al Makkah General Store is a premier subsidiary of Bukhari NEXUS Pvt Ltd. We specialize in the high-volume supply of essential household and commercial goods, maintaining a focus on affordability, consistent availability, and superior quality for our B2B and retail partners.

Comprehensive Product Portfolio

Grocery Essentials: Premium Rice, Flour, Sugar, Spices, Oils, and Lentils.
Beverage Solutions: Full range of soft drinks, juices, bottled water, and tea varieties.
Household & Hygiene: Cleaning agents, laundry essentials, and professional kitchenware.
Personal Care: Skincare, soaps, shampoos, and high-demand hygiene products.

Strategic Expansion & Future Growth

We are aggressively scaling our operations to transition into a regional FMCG powerhouse:

  • Distribution Networks: Building a robust, multi-channel regional supply chain.
  • Dealer Programs: Launching formalized dealership programs for local retailers.
  • Logistics Infrastructure: Investing in large-scale climate-controlled warehousing.
  • Digital Integration: Implementing a B2B digital ordering portal for our commercial partners.
