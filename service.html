Agricultural Supply & Agri-Commodity Trading

Bukhari Traders

Bukhari Traders operates as a high-capacity commodity origination and supply chain hub specializing in critical grain economies. Our core business centers on the end-to-end management, collection, storage, and wholesale circulation of essential agricultural yields, prioritizing high-grade paddy, wheat, and refined rice stocks.

  • Direct Farmer Origination: Partnering directly with local growers at the farm-gate level to source raw paddy and wheat directly upon harvest.
  • Commission House Networks: Interfacing with regional commission shops and wholesale brokerage desks to acquire heavily aggregated, high-volume yields.
  • Small Trader Synergy: Partnering with independent local merchants to consolidate distributed grain collections into bulk industrial-sized reserves.
Core Operations

• Bulk Crop Sourcing & Accumulation
• Strategic Wheat & Paddy Storage
• Industrial Rice Placement
